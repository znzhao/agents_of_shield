S.H.I.E.L.D.'s core team has been relentlessly pursued through history by the Chronicoms and Nathaniel Malick, who have been systematically assaulting the organization and altering key events to erase S.H.I.E.L.D. from existence. The team's time-hopping efforts to combat these threats have taken a heavy toll, most notably the loss of trusted ally Enoch and repeated moral compromises, further strained by Simmons' enforced memory loss regarding Fitz's secret whereabouts and plan. As secrets and trust issues fester, Daisy has uncovered painful truths about her lineage, including the murder of her mother Jiaying at Malick's hands—an act designed to wound Daisy and manipulate her estranged sister Kora.

Malick, with Chronicom Sybil's guidance and aided by John Garrett and a faction of Inhuman recruits, has grown in power, targeting S.H.I.E.L.D.'s history and inciting chaos. Malick's manipulation of Kora—torn between her newfound family with Daisy and the allure of changing the timeline—threatens the team's unity, especially as the Zephyr One is commandeered and taken into space. Onboard, Malick attempts to extract Fitz's location from Simmons, but her memory implant thwarts him, to the point where Simmons herself forgets Fitz entirely. Back on Earth, Kora oscillates between sides, offering to aid S.H.I.E.L.D. but ultimately being recaptured by Malick. Sibyl, now in the Lighthouse systems, orchestrates mayhem, further demonstrating the Chronicoms' infiltration capabilities and elevating the stakes as the team's losses mount and the final confrontation looms, with Fitz's fate still the crucial unknown.
