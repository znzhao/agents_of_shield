The agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. have been pursuing the Chronicoms through different time periods, striving to protect the timeline from enemy manipulation. In their journey, they've been forced into moral compromises, including actions that ensure the rise of Hydra in order to preserve the future, and the elimination of key figures like Wilfred Malick. The repeated trauma and losses—culminating in Mack's parents being murdered and replaced by Chronicom duplicates—have frayed the team’s cohesion and morale. Daisy and Sousa, growing closer amid adversity, were both subjected to horrific experimentation when Nathaniel Malick attempted to steal Daisy's Inhuman abilities. Meanwhile, Simmons has used a memory device to conceal Fitz's location and guidance, straining her own mental stability and the team's trust in her. Coulson, ever the sacrificial leader, managed to destroy the Chronicom Hunters using his own artificial life, after a confrontation with their Predictor, Sibyl.

After a catastrophic time jump strands Mack and Deke in 1982, Mack succumbs to grief, isolating himself and pushing Deke away. Deke, determined to maintain hope, adapts to the era and builds an eccentric but dedicated team called the "Deke Squad," using their band as a front for a makeshift intelligence operation. During this period, Coulson reemerges as an A.I. housed in a TV, assisting as best he can. Sibyl, having survived Coulson’s sabotage, creates rudimentary Chronicom threats and orchestrates a new attack on the team to recover the vital Time Stream. Several members of Deke's new team are killed, but Mack, inspired by Deke's persistent care, commits to leading the ragtag squad and together they repel the Chronicom attackers—though Sibyl escapes with the Time Stream. Mack, recovering some sense of purpose, reconnects with Deke and finds enough closure to visit his younger self and brother from afar, offering a silent gift to his past. Even as the fractured team and new allies regroup, the Chronicoms remain at large, in possession of dangerous knowledge, with the timeline still dangerously unstable and the original S.H.I.E.L.D. team scattered across eras.
