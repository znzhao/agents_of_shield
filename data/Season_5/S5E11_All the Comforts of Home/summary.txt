After being transported to a dystopian future where Earth is shattered and humanity is enslaved by the Kree, the S.H.I.E.L.D. team fights to survive and return home. They are forced to confront harrowing losses, with Simmons subjugated by the Kree leader Kasius, Daisy stripped of her powers and imprisoned, and Yo-Yo subjected to gruesome Kree experimentation. Fitz executes a rescue mission from the past, helping the team rally an uprising with the help of Flint, a young Inhuman whose powers become pivotal. Using cryptic clues from Robin, a prophetic child, the team learns that reversing Earth's fate hinges on escaping this timeline and altering history.

Their rebellion succeeds at great cost: Enoch and Deke sacrifice themselves to power a reconstructed Monolith, opening a time portal that returns the core team to the present, leaving the future in flames and Flint rallying survivors. Back in the present, S.H.I.E.L.D. regroups in the abandoned Lighthouse bunker—now revealed to be a forgotten Cold War relic—where they discover it holds not only resources but also new threats. Deke unexpectedly survives the Monolith's jump, bewildered by the present-day world. Meanwhile, S.H.I.E.L.D.'s return triggers General Hale's hunt, and a trap set by former ally Piper results in an ambush by robotic soldiers led by the masked Ruby, Hale's daughter. In the chaos, Yo-Yo is gravely injured—her arms severed, echoing a fate revealed in the future. A sacrificial act by the Chronicom Noah saves Daisy and Fitz from a Kree beacon's explosion, while tensions and betrayals deepen the mystery of Hale and Ruby's true motives. The team is battered but determined, aware that just returning to the present may not be enough to prevent Earth's impending destruction.
