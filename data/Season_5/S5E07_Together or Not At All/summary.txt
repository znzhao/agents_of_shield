The S.H.I.E.L.D. team is hurled into a brutal future where Earth is shattered and the remnants of humanity cling to survival within the Kree-ruled Lighthouse station. The Kree, led by the cruel Kasius and his enforcer Sinara, seek to profit from the forced evolution and exploitation of Inhumans. The team is initially separated: Simmons is enslaved as Kasius’ servant, Daisy is branded the feared "Destroyer of Worlds" but rendered powerless, and May is cast to the surface with a grievous injury. Fitz, left behind in the present, devises a risky plan with Lance Hunter and Enoch the Chronicom to follow the team into the future, where he dramatically reunites with Simmons. Ben, a telepathic Inhuman, is executed for defying Kasius, and Tess is killed after aiding the team, losses that harden the group’s resolve.

In the Lighthouse, Flint, a young Inhuman, awakens to his powers and joins the fractured group after avenging Tess by killing a cruel overseer. Tensions among the Kree escalate when Kasius’ brother Faulnak arrives, undermining Kasius and revealing their family's disdain since the brothers once abandoned their people in battle. Trapped, Daisy, Fitz, and Simmons try to escape but are thwarted when the Kree destroy their exit ship and send the formidable Maston-Dar after them. Fitz is wounded, and the escapees are forced to team up with Deke and reunite with Coulson, Mack, Yo-Yo, and Flint. Flint proves his loyalty and growing confidence, using his powers to kill the Kree Vicar and later barricade the team from their pursuers. Sinara’s alliance shifts when she kills Maston-Dar, and Kasius murders Faulnak to seize control of their fate and solidify his legacy.

Meanwhile, May survives terrors on the devastated surface thanks to Enoch, only to be captured by a mysterious inhabitant. With the lighthouse group now regathered, battered but determined, the central mysteries remain: how to return to their own time, reverse the destruction of Earth, and fulfill Inhuman prophet Robin's enigmatic visions. Alliances among the Kree are fracturing, and S.H.I.E.L.D. stands united, ready to challenge both their oppressors and the shadow of the future that hangs over them all.
