After stopping Aida and escaping the Framework, the core S.H.I.E.L.D. team—Coulson, Daisy, May, Simmons, Mack, and Yo-Yo—are mysteriously transported to a shattered future Earth governed by Kree overlords in a space habitat known as the Lighthouse. Humanity survives here in brutal, tightly controlled conditions, with daily life overshadowed by harsh labor, ritualistic killings called Renewal, and the systematic erasure of history. As the team attempts to blend in, they rely on scavenger Deke and local survivor Tess for guidance, but quickly recognize the dangers imposed by the Kree: Simmons is captured and pressed into service by Kasius, the Kree leader, who forces her to assist with the presentation of Inhumans for trade or entertainment.

Simmons is tasked with helping a young Inhuman, Abby, learn to control her new powers before being thrown into a gladiatorial fight for Lady Basha, a high-status Kree buyer. Despite Simmons' moral outrage and efforts to give Abby a chance, the brutal system prevails; Abby, frightened and barely in control, is forced to kill her opponent, subsequently sold off to Basha. Throughout, Simmons' compassion is continually at odds with the Kree's cruelty, and her safety grows more precarious. Elsewhere, Coulson's group tries to uncover the significance of clues left by Virgil, finding a hidden radio that may provide answers, though their actions are hampered by Grill's suspicions and harsh oversight. They are forced to knock out Grill’s enforcer rather than kill him, emphasizing their commitment to their own ideals despite mounting adversity.

Meanwhile, Yo-Yo and Daisy take greater risks: Yo-Yo steals a Kree tablet for Daisy, who attempts a bold rescue mission to find Simmons but is ultimately betrayed by Deke, who valued survival over aiding her rebellion. As Daisy is captured and her feared reputation as "Quake, Destroyer of Worlds" comes to light within the Lighthouse, the S.H.I.E.L.D. team finds themselves isolated and endangered on multiple fronts, still searching for a way home and a means to inspire hope in a future ruled by despair. Trust, survival, and the cost of resistance define their present circumstances, as the full horror of Earth’s fate and Kree oppression begin to emerge.
