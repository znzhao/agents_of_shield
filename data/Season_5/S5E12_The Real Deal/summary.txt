The S.H.I.E.L.D. team, after being transported to a devastated future Earth enslaved by the Kree, led an uprising and returned to their present, but not without consequences. Their time-jump triggered by the destruction of the Monoliths unleashed unpredictable space-time anomalies in the Lighthouse bunker. The team must now contend not only with General Hale's relentless hunt and Ruby's deadly ambushes but also with the disorienting presence of Deke, a survivor from the future adapting to this timeline.

Recent events have brought personal and physical harm: Yo-Yo was gravely injured, losing her arms in an attack echoing the future's horrors, deepening her bond with Mack. As the team uncovers the source of new anomalies—a rupture in space-time caused by the simultaneous destruction of three Monoliths—they face manifestations of their worst fears, from forests appearing underground to deadly apparitions like Lash and Hive. Coulson, whose secret deterioration is revealed by Simmons, volunteers to close the rift at personal risk, recognizing his time is limited due to a fatal aftereffect of his bargain with the Ghost Rider. The team, supported by allies like Deathlok, rallies to seal the rift, but with Earth's destruction still looming and the nature of reality itself in question, S.H.I.E.L.D. is battered, united, and more desperate than ever to avert catastrophe.
