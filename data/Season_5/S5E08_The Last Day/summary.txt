The S.H.I.E.L.D. team is thrust into a fractured future where Earth has been shattered and humanity is oppressed under Kree rule within the remnants of the Lighthouse station. The team arrives separated and disoriented: Simmons falls into Kasius’s hands as a mute servant, Daisy is imprisoned and disempowered, and May is banished to the Earth’s ruined surface. Fitz manages to reach the future by freezing himself, reuniting dramatically with Simmons and helping orchestrate several escape attempts. Alongside Deke and Flint—a newly emergent Inhuman—the group unites, enduring deadly Kree schemes, Sinara's shifting allegiances, and the constant threat of betrayal and punishment. Flint’s powers become crucial as the team begins to resist the Kree, culminating in the death of Kasius’s brother and several Kree enforcers, and in organizing a stand within the Lighthouse to protect and free remaining human survivors.

Meanwhile, Robin Hinton, the Inhuman seer, becomes a pivotal but enigmatic figure, burdened by visions scattered across her lifetime. Flashbacks and present-day scenes reveal that after the Earth's destruction, surviving S.H.I.E.L.D. members—May, FitzSimmons, Yo-Yo—struggled in the Lighthouse, raising Robin amidst despair while trying in vain to build a time machine guided by her prophecies. These visions, however, are fragmented and often inscrutable even to Robin herself, leaving the team with only incomplete guidance on how to change the timeline or return home. The situation deteriorates as the Kree clamp down on the remaining humans, unleashing Vrellnexian roaches as punishment, while in the surface outpost, Deke learns his father died under mysterious circumstances.

In the present, S.H.I.E.L.D. struggles to extract clear information from Robin about restoring the timeline. Tragedy strikes when scavengers, fearing Daisy as the Earth's destroyer, attempt to kill her, but instead mortally wound Robin before she can disclose the path home. In her final moments, Robin reveals to May—a surrogate mother in this timeline—the crucial secret for their return, cryptically referencing Flint. With the team battered and reeling from fresh loss, they realize that the key to returning home and saving the world may rest with Flint, whose significance is only now becoming clear.
