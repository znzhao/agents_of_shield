The S.H.I.E.L.D. team has been transported to a future where Earth is shattered and humanity survives under brutal Kree rule in the Lighthouse space station. The Kree, led by Kasius and his enforcer Sinara, exploit humans—especially Inhumans, who are forced into gladiatorial combat and sold to alien bidders. The team is torn apart: Simmons is made Kasius’ servant, Daisy is seized as the infamous 'Destroyer of Worlds,' and May, gravely wounded, is captured after fighting in the arena. Deke, a survivor from this era, betrays Daisy hoping to protect the community, deepening tensions. Meanwhile, the Kree are breeding and evaluating human children for potential Inhuman abilities. Tess, a young woman aiding the team, bravely stands up for them but ultimately falls victim to Kree punishment for her defiance.

Back in the present, Fitz is left behind, captured and interrogated by authorities. He learns of the team's fate and, with Lance Hunter and the Chronicom Enoch’s help, uncovers Robin Hinton—a young Inhuman Seer whose visions of Earth's destruction prompted their abduction. Fitz infiltrates a military facility and travels alone to the future to reunite with his friends and alter humanity’s doomed timeline.

Upon reuniting with Simmons, Fitz infiltrates Kasius’ auction disguised as a ruthless marauder, seeking to free his teammates. Tensions among the Kree escalate when Kasius’ brother Faulnak arrives, challenging his authority. During an elaborate arena exhibition attended by intergalactic guests, Ben—Daisy’s telepathic ally—is executed for betrayal, and Flint, a newly-emerged Inhuman, kills their overseer Grill while protecting his friends. Using subterfuge, fighting, and a surge of courage, Fitz and Simmons rescue Daisy; together, the trio escape while May is sent to the surface and the remaining team reels from the loss of Tess, vowing to protect Flint and uphold their mission prophesied by Robin—to save what remains of the world.
