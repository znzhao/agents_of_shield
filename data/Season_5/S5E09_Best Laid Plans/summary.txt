After being transported into a dystopian future where the shattered remains of Earth and its surviving human population are oppressed by the Kree in the Lighthouse, the S.H.I.E.L.D. team endures separation and hardship before gradually reuniting. Simmons is initially forced to serve the Kree leader Kasius in silence, Daisy is imprisoned and stripped of her powers, and May is exiled to the devastated Earth's surface. Fitz arrives in the future via cryogenic stasis, orchestrating jailbreaks and fueling the group's struggle against the Kree. The emergence of Flint, an Inhuman with the ability to control rocks, marks a major turning point as he becomes central to their fight back. Together with allies like Deke, Tess, and the traumatized FitzSimmons, they engineer bold plans to shield and liberate the remaining humans, suffering losses and betrayals while navigating the ruthless politics and violent enforcement of Kasius and his minions.

Amidst the oppressive regime, Robin Hinton, an Inhuman seer, provides scattered prophecies that muddle the team's understanding of how to change the future or return home. The situation worsens with attacks from both the Kree and human scavengers, one of which fatally wounds Robin. Before dying, Robin reveals cryptic clues to May that emphasize Flint's unique role as the key to their return. As the S.H.I.E.L.D. agents rally the Lighthouse resistance, Mack, Yo-Yo, and Flint mount an open revolt, facing off against Kree Reapers and foiling Kasius’s plan to bomb the humans by outmaneuvering him with a clever bluff using Terrigen resources. Meanwhile, Coulson and the others aboard Zephyr One fight to ready the ship for return, surviving a treacherous ride through gravity storms thanks to Simmons’ quick thinking. Daisy defeats Sinara during a deadly skirmish on the ship. The Zephyr successfully contacts the rebels in the Lighthouse, setting the stage for the reunited team's final push and hinting that their escape—and the prevention of Earth’s destruction—will depend on Flint's powers and choices.
