After battling the threat of Aida and her LMD and Framework operations, S.H.I.E.L.D. was left discredited and on the run, their name further tarnished as they were framed for terrorist acts, including the attempted assassination of General Talbot. The team managed to defeat Aida with the help of Ghost Rider, but at great emotional cost, particularly for Fitz, who struggles with guilt over his dark actions in the virtual world. Despite their victory, the agents were swiftly apprehended by mysterious operatives wielding advanced technology, and Fitz was left behind as the rest of the team disappeared into custody.

Unexpectedly, Coulson, Daisy, May, Mack, Simmons, and Yo-Yo awaken in a strange facility where an alien monolith transports them to a remote space outpost controlled by the Kree. Disoriented, the team attempts to piece together their situation, only to learn, through cryptic clues and interactions with locals like Virgil and Deke, that not only are they in deep space—they have also apparently traveled into the future. On this outpost, remnants of humanity struggle to survive under Kree oppression and the constant threat of monstrous "roaches." With the Earth revealed to be catastrophically fragmented, the agents realize they are the legendary figures expected to save what remains of humanity. As they struggle to survive and make sense of their new reality, the team is divided and under threat, searching for any way to communicate with Fitz and to understand their role in this broken future.
