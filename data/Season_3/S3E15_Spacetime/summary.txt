The resurgence of Hydra under the ancient Inhuman entity now possessing Grant Ward's body has ignited a global crisis. Led by Gideon Malick and deeply infiltrated into political and corporate power structures, Hydra aims to build a new world order by manipulating governments and targeting Inhumans for experimentation and recruitment. S.H.I.E.L.D., reeling from the forced exile of Bobbi Morse and Lance Hunter after a failed mission, faces growing public paranoia, government crackdowns, and terror attacks from the anti-Inhuman extremist group, the Watchdogs. Team members struggle with losses and shifting relationships: Melinda May grieves over losing her ex-husband, Dr. Andrew Garner, to his irreversible transformation into the monstrous Lash, while Daisy Johnson emerges as a determined protector and advocate for her fellow Inhumans, forging new alliances with powered allies like Yo-Yo Rodriguez. Ethical quandaries, like the development of an Inhuman cure from Creel’s blood, create internal tension, especially as Daisy resists its use.

Hydra's agenda escalates when they abduct Charles Hinton, an Inhuman whose touch grants others visions of the future. After a Brooklyn encounter predicted in a vision comes to pass despite S.H.I.E.L.D.'s intervention, Daisy herself receives a traumatic glimpse of catastrophes still to come—including the apparent death of a S.H.I.E.L.D. agent in a space shuttle explosion. Hydra exploits Charlie's powers and strong-arms the Transia Corporation, acquiring powerful exoskeleton technology for Malick. Meanwhile, Andrew Garner returns to S.H.I.E.L.D. in his final moments of humanity to say goodbye to May, choosing to be locked away during his last transformation into Lash. Despite the team's efforts and attempts to challenge fate, Charlie is ultimately killed after ensuring Daisy receives one final, ominous vision, raising the stakes around both Hydra's technological ambitions and the threat of an irreversible future. Coulson’s team, battered and haunted by what they cannot change, remains resolute but increasingly isolated as the struggle between S.H.I.E.L.D., Hydra, and a fearful public intensifies.
