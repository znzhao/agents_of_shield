The threat of Hive, inhabiting Grant Ward’s body, has brought S.H.I.E.L.D. to its breaking point. Hive’s power to enslave Inhumans led to Daisy’s turn against her friends, causing devastating emotional fallout and a rift in the team. S.H.I.E.L.D. scrambled to cure Hive’s influence and counter his grotesque experiments to transform humans into primeval Inhumans using Kree DNA. While the pressure from the Sokovia Accords and General Talbot forced S.H.I.E.L.D. to navigate government oversight, the team’s focus remained on stopping Hive’s apocalyptic scheme. Lash, the Inhuman killer, freed Daisy from Hive’s thrall at the cost of his own life, allowing Daisy to return to S.H.I.E.L.D. wracked with guilt. Meanwhile, relationships within the team deepened, notably between Fitz and Simmons, and Yo-Yo began to find her place among them, while Lincoln, torn by love and loyalty, became increasingly isolated.

Hive’s plan to trigger global Inhuman transformation came to a head when he seized a missile silo and prepared to launch a warhead laced with Dr. Radcliffe’s pathogen. S.H.I.E.L.D. mounted a desperate assault: Coulson and Talbot orchestrated a bureaucratic gambit to obtain the launch abort codes, and May, Mack, Lincoln, and Elena infiltrated the silo. The team managed to stall the launch and used the T.A.H.I.T.I. memory device to disorient Hive, then contained him in a gel matrix. Dr. Radcliffe was taken into custody to help reverse the primitive transformation. However, Hive’s loyalists executed a hidden plan—"Absolution"—planting a pathogen-filled bomb in a shipping crate at S.H.I.E.L.D.'s base. The bomb’s release unleashed a wave of primitive transformation within S.H.I.E.L.D. and forced a frantic scramble to contain the infected. Daisy, suffering acute withdrawal from Hive’s influence and bearing immense guilt, warned of Hive’s ongoing danger. Now, with Hive and Radcliffe in captivity but S.H.I.E.L.D. compromised from within, the team faces the imminent collapse of their sanctuary and the unresolved threat of Hive’s followers continuing his catastrophic endgame.
