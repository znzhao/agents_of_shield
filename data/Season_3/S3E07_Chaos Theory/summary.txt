After the collapse of Hydra and the public exposure of S.H.I.E.L.D., a wave of Terrigen Mist has awakened countless new Inhumans across the globe. Coulson’s restructured S.H.I.E.L.D. struggles to operate from the shadows while partnering with the ATCU, a government task force led by Rosalind Price, to manage the Inhuman crisis. Tensions simmer between S.H.I.E.L.D.’s commitment to protecting Inhumans and the ATCU’s more draconian containment and stasis policies. Daisy Johnson emerges as a passionate advocate for her fellow Inhumans, clashing with both the public's fear and S.H.I.E.L.D.'s uneasy allies.

Parallel to this, a monstrous figure known as Lash is executing Inhumans, targeting them with ruthless precision. The team’s investigation uncovers Lash’s true identity in a shocking turn: Dr. Andrew Garner, May’s ex-husband, underwent accidental Terrigenesis and has been desperately fighting his transformation into the killer Inhuman within. May confronts Andrew, torn between love and duty, ultimately subduing him with help from the team and permitting the ATCU to place him in stasis to halt his irreversible change into Lash. 

Meanwhile, Fitz rescues Simmons from an alien world, only to learn of her harrowing survival with astronaut Will Daniels and her deep emotional scars. Their relationship becomes more complicated as they work together to decipher clues that could allow for a return rescue mission. As Coulson navigates a fragile truce with Rosalind and Hydra regroups under Grant Ward, S.H.I.E.L.D. is beset by both external threats and the mounting cost of secrets and transformations within its own ranks.
