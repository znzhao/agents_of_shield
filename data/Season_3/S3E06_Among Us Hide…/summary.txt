After the collapse of Hydra and the exposure of S.H.I.E.L.D., a global crisis emerges as Terrigen awakens many new Inhumans, compelling Coulson's team to regroup and operate under the radar amidst growing government scrutiny. Daisy Johnson (Quake) has taken the lead in recruiting and helping fellow Inhumans, despite public fear and the threat of the murderous Lash — a monstrous being hunting down Inhumans. Meanwhile, S.H.I.E.L.D. forms a fraught alliance with the ATCU, a government agency led by Rosalind Price, which seeks to control and 'cure' Inhumans, keeping them sedated in secret facilities. Tensions rise as Daisy and her allies investigate both Lash's true identity and the ATCU's methods, suspecting corruption or hidden motives within the agency.

Simultaneously, the personal stakes increase: Fitz rescues Simmons from an alien planet after months of isolation, learning of her survival alongside Will Daniels, a stranded NASA astronaut. Simmons and Fitz’s bond deepens as they vow to mount a rescue mission for Will, complicated by lingering romantic tensions. On other fronts, Grant Ward begins to rebuild Hydra, orchestrating attempts on S.H.I.E.L.D. agents — including an attack on Dr. Andrew Garner, May’s estranged husband. The fallout fractures team trust: Hunter’s reckless pursuit of Ward leads to Andrew’s near-death, leaving May furious and determined to take matters into her own hands, with Bobbi rejoins her for the mission.

Key revelations accelerate the plot: Daisy and Mack discover Lash is hiding in human form and suspect a connection to ATCU's Banks, but their investigation finds no proof. During an assault on a Hydra safe house, Bobbi regains her confidence in battle, while May interrogates Werner von Strucker. In his dying moments, Strucker confesses that Dr. Garner transformed into a monstrous killer — revealing that Lash is, in fact, Andrew Garner. This shocking truth upends the team's understanding of their enemy and personal safety, setting the stage for new dangers and betrayals as S.H.I.E.L.D. faces both internal and external threats.
