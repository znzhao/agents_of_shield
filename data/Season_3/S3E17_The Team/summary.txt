Driven by repeated threats from Hydra and the unleashing of the ancient Inhuman Hive—now inhabiting the body of Grant Ward—S.H.I.E.L.D. has endured devastating betrayals, brutal sacrifices, and mounting paranoia. Hydra, under Hive’s control, has manipulated Inhumans and tried to ignite global chaos. Gideon Malick, Hydra’s power broker, lost his daughter and legacy to Hive’s vengeance, exposing his lifelong guilt and fracturing his resolve. S.H.I.E.L.D., under Coulson’s leadership, faces strained loyalties after the banishment of Bobbi and Hunter, and the anti-Inhuman fervor stoked by the Watchdogs continues to put powered individuals at risk. Daisy Johnson, grappling with newfound leadership and responsibility for her fellow Inhumans, recruits allies like Elena Rodriguez and Joey Gutierrez to form the Secret Warriors.

When Coulson's team is captured by Hydra, Daisy assembles the Secret Warriors, who successfully rescue them and capture Malick. However, Malick warns that Hive can now infect and control Inhumans, sowing seeds of suspicion within S.H.I.E.L.D. Tensions erupt as evidence surfaces that one of the Secret Warriors is under Hive’s sway, leading to suspicion, containment, and the fracturing of trust between S.H.I.E.L.D. and their Inhuman agents. The truth emerges tragically: Daisy herself is Hive’s sleeper agent. Under his influence, she murders Malick, attempts to free Lincoln, and then violently escapes, stealing vital S.H.I.E.L.D. resources and leaving their base devastated by her earthquake powers. Now, S.H.I.E.L.D. is both weakened and fractured, with the fate of the Secret Warriors in turmoil, trust at a breaking point, and Hive’s threat more insidious than ever.
