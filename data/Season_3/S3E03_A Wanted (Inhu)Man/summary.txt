After the fall of Hydra and S.H.I.E.L.D.'s public exposure, Phil Coulson leads a covert team in a tense world where Inhumans are manifesting unpredictably due to the Terrigen outbreak. The team strives to protect and support these empowered individuals while facing competition and suspicion from the government-run ATCU, led by Rosalind Price. Daisy Johnson (Skye), embracing her powers and new role, aims to unite Inhumans under a shield of protection, but S.H.I.E.L.D. is accused of criminal acts and forced to operate from the shadows. Leo Fitz’s determination and scientific prowess enabled the spectacular rescue of Jemma Simmons from an alien planet, though her psychological trauma and the mysteries surrounding her ordeal linger unresolved.

Lincoln, a powerful but troubled Inhuman, becomes the primary target of a nationwide ATCU manhunt after his powers and presence become public knowledge. While S.H.I.E.L.D. attempts to help him, Lincoln’s mistrust leads to chaos and tragedy, ultimately forcing him back into hiding after a confrontation and a desperate act that results in an accidental death. Amid the standoff, Coulson chooses diplomacy, offering himself to the ATCU in exchange for Daisy’s safety and a chance to collaborate. Meanwhile, Lance Hunter and Agent May are on a brutal undercover mission to infiltrate Hydra and get to Grant Ward, their quest marked by violence, deception, and growing personal costs. As the conflict with Hydra intensifies, S.H.I.E.L.D. grapples with internal strains, shifting alliances, and the looming danger of more Inhumans emerging uncontrolled. The fate of Simmons’s recovery and Lincoln’s future, along with the true goals of both ATCU and Hydra, remain urgent, unresolved threads.
