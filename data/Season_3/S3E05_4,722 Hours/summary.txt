After Hydra's downfall and S.H.I.E.L.D.'s exposure, the world reels from a surge of newly awakened Inhumans, sparked by the global spread of Terrigen. Coulson's team operates under clandestine pressure, navigating fragile alliances and conflicts with the government-run ATCU, led by Rosalind Price, who seeks to control and contain Inhuman emergence. Daisy Johnson, herself an empowered Inhuman, spearheads efforts to locate and support others like her, though fear, mistrust, and a terrifying serial killer known as Lash, who hunts Inhumans, complicate her mission. Lash's true nature and close connection to S.H.I.E.L.D. remain mysterious, as the team uncovers he can transform into human form, escalating their fears.

Fitz mounts a desperate operation to rescue Jemma Simmons, presumed lost after being swallowed by the alien Kree monolith. Simmons' mysterious return leaves her traumatized but physically unharmed, withholding the details of her ordeal. Meanwhile, the struggle against Hydra intensifies, with Grant Ward launching a vicious campaign to rebuild the shattered organization. Hunter and May, intent on revenge, attempt to assassinate Ward but become ensnared in his schemes, placing May's ex-husband Dr. Andrew Garner in mortal danger, his fate uncertain.

Simmons finally reveals what befell her during her months of disappearance: transported to a desolate, sunless alien world, she survived against the odds, eventually encountering Will Daniels, a NASA astronaut marooned for over a decade. Together, they contended with a malevolent entity haunting the planet, developed a deep bond, and repeatedly attempted—and failed—to return home. Simmons' rescue by Fitz comes only after profound psychological and emotional struggle, including a brief romantic connection with Will. In the wake of her story, Fitz silently dedicates himself to helping Simmons rescue Will, uniting them in a new, urgent quest even as their feelings for each other are complicated by what’s been discovered. Unresolved dangers, especially the nature of the alien entity and the threat posed by Lash and Hydra, shadow the team's future.
