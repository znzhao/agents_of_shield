The fall of S.H.I.E.L.D. at Hydra’s hands left the organization fractured into splintered loyalties and rival factions. Coulson became Director, struggling to hold together his team and respond to threats both external and internal. Their quest to understand the origins of the alien artifact known as the Diviner drew them into dangerous conflict with Hydra and revealed the existence of the Inhumans, who possess latent powers unlocked by Terrigenesis. After being exposed to the Terrigen crystal, Skye developed seismic powers, though she struggled to control them—leading to accidental tragedy and deep mistrust from those closest to her, especially Simmons. Meanwhile, Fitz has concealed Skye’s altered DNA to protect her, while Ward—now a rogue element—follows his own violent vendettas with the unstable Agent 33.

New ideological rifts fractured S.H.I.E.L.D. further, as Mack and Bobbi Morse were revealed to be part of an alternate "real S.H.I.E.L.D." led by Robert Gonzales, which believes Coulson’s association with alien technology makes him unfit for command. Flashbacks reveal that this faction resisted orders to destroy a S.H.I.E.L.D. aircraft carrier after Hydra’s infiltration, choosing instead to save their fellow agents, forging a bond of loyalty. In the present, Gonzales' forces launch a coup, seizing control of Coulson’s base—the Playground—through deception and force, making prisoners of Coulson’s core team. May narrowly escapes and aids Coulson's escape.

Meanwhile, Skye’s isolation in a hidden safehouse leaves her emotionally and physically vulnerable as she grapples with her new abilities and sense of self. She is approached by Gordon, an Inhuman who offers guidance but leaves when she hesitates. When Gonzales’s agents attack her in an attempt to neutralize the threat she poses, Skye loses control and severely wounds an agent, horrified by her power. She calls out to Gordon, who whisks her away, leaving her future and allegiance uncertain. As the hostile "real S.H.I.E.L.D." consolidates its power, the original team is scattered and the fate of both S.H.I.E.L.D. and the emerging Inhuman community hangs in the balance.
