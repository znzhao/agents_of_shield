Following the fall of S.H.I.E.L.D., Coulson's team has been relentlessly hunted by Hydra and global authorities, with trust eroded among allies. The alien Obelisk (Diviner) has come to the fore as the focus of both S.H.I.E.L.D. and Hydra's ambitions—its secrets tied to Skye's mysterious heritage and the alien compulsions driving Coulson. Through a string of murders and investigation, Coulson realizes the alien symbols haunting him are not just a map but blueprints to a secret city, accessible only to those the Diviner deems worthy. The mental toll on Coulson deepens tensions within the team, while Skye is forced to confront disturbing truths about her own origins and her father, whose violent, erratic search for her has brought him into alliance with their enemies.

Dr. Whitehall's true identity as Hydra scientist Dr. Werner Reinhardt is revealed, and his unnaturally prolonged life links back to WWII and brutal experiments with the Diviner, including harvesting the organs of an ageless woman who survives contact with the object. In the present, Whitehall forms an uneasy alliance with Skye's father (Cal), shifting Hydra’s focus from weaponizing the Obelisk to unlocking the ancient city's power. Meanwhile, Grant Ward executes his long-planned revenge against his family, exposing and then murdering his brother, Senator Christian Ward, as well as their parents—framing it as a murder-suicide and fully severing his ties to his old life. Ward leverages his knowledge of S.H.I.E.L.D. to rejoin Hydra, becoming a dangerous wild card.

On S.H.I.E.L.D.'s side, Bobbi Morse and Simmons dig into Hydra’s past and learn of Whitehall's history, nearly losing Hydra enforcer Bakshi after a brutal interrogation. Fitz slowly adapts to his cognitive impairment with Mack’s support, while old wounds and suspicions among the team members persist. After a dangerous run-in with Cal, who manipulates Coulson during a mission and nearly kills Agent Triplett, the team finally locates the ancient city—and realizes Hydra, armed with Cal’s insights, is now racing them there. Most mysteries of the alien connection, the temple, and Skye's true nature remain unresolved, as both S.H.I.E.L.D. and Hydra hurtle toward a fateful confrontation beneath the surface.
