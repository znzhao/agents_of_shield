The collapse of S.H.I.E.L.D. following Hydra’s secret infiltration has forced Coulson and his remaining loyal agents—May, Skye, Fitz, Simmons, and newcomer Hunter—underground, struggling to survive, rebuild, and resist Hydra’s ongoing power grab. Trust remains fragile, scarred by Grant Ward’s betrayal and Fitz’s traumatic brain injury, which has left him struggling mentally and emotionally. Simmons, initially absent, is revealed to be operating undercover within Hydra, providing vital intelligence while walking a razor’s edge to maintain her cover. Fitz, feeling abandoned, finds small solace in the support of Mack.

Hydra, led by the methodical and ruthless Daniel Whitehall, aggressively pursues powerful assets and talent, notably through the brainwashing "Faustus method." After S.H.I.E.L.D.’s failed attempt to secure the dangerous Obelisk—an artifact tied to Skye’s mysterious heritage—Whitehall and his agent Bakshi continue recruiting former S.H.I.E.L.D. agents and enhanced individuals. Simmons is nearly exposed when Hydra seeks to reacquire Donnie Gill, a former S.H.I.E.L.D. student turned dangerous superhuman able to freeze with a touch. Their operation reveals Hydra’s chilling success in brainwashing, leading Skye to take her first lethal shot to prevent Gill from killing Simmons and furthering Hydra’s plans.

Coulson’s team manages to salvage technology from Hydra’s operations but continues to face internal strains. Ward remains imprisoned, providing insight into Hydra’s methods but serving as a constant reminder of past betrayals, especially for Fitz, who nearly kills him in a fit of trauma-induced rage. Meanwhile, Skye grows into her role as an agent—training with May and making hard decisions in the field—even as she grapples with her unknown origins and the secret of Coulson’s strange compulsions. Hydra, ever-adaptive, identifies Simmons as a valuable asset, not fully suspecting her true loyalties, but considers tightening their psychological control over her, heightening the danger as S.H.I.E.L.D. and Hydra engage in a deadly game of espionage and survival.
