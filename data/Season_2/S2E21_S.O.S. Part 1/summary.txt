After Hydra's exposure within S.H.I.E.L.D. and the organization's subsequent collapse, new power struggles emerge, notably between Phil Coulson and Robert Gonzales, each representing differing philosophies on leadership and security. Skye, now embracing her identity as Daisy Johnson, discovers her Inhuman heritage through her reunion with her parents: Jiaying, leader of an isolated Inhuman community, and her unstable father Cal. As both S.H.I.E.L.D. and the Inhumans become targets of Hydra's experimentation, they briefly form an alliance to rescue captured allies. However, unity is temporary, eroded by residual mistrust, especially following Ward's repeated betrayals and the secretive Theta Protocol initiative.

Attempts at peace between S.H.I.E.L.D. and the Inhumans disintegrate catastrophically after Jiaying murders Gonzales during negotiations and manipulates events to frame S.H.I.E.L.D. as aggressors, provoking open conflict. Distrust and fear escalate on both sides; key agents like Simmons and Mack display increased skepticism and independence, while core relationships—such as Skye's torn loyalties between her biological and found families—are strained to breaking. Unbeknownst to most, Jiaying is orchestrating a war for her own ends, while Raina's new precognitive abilities enable her to foresee and attempt to warn against Jiaying’s machinations, only to be killed for her efforts.

As Coulson scrambles to defend S.H.I.E.L.D.'s assets and people, Inhumans, led by Jiaying, launch coordinated assaults—stealthily seizing control of the Iliad aircraft carrier and capturing Skye after she uncovers her mother's deception and resorts to confrontation. Meanwhile, personal vendettas play out as Ward and Agent 33 torture and endanger Bobbi Morse in pursuit of retribution, further destabilizing S.H.I.E.L.D.'s extended team. Amid these battles, the true purposes of hidden projects (like the Kree weapon and Theta Protocol) and the fates of several major characters—including a now-captive Skye—remain unresolved, as S.H.I.E.L.D. faces the existential threat of war with superpowered Inhumans from within and without.
