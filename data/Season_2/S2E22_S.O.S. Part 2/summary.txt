Following the exposure and fall of S.H.I.E.L.D. after Hydra’s infiltration, leadership battles have fractured the organization, pitting Phil Coulson against Robert Gonzales’s faction. Throughout these upheavals, Skye, now embracing her identity as Daisy Johnson, uncovers her Inhuman heritage and reunites with her estranged biological parents: Jiaying, who leads an isolated community of Inhumans, and Cal, whose unstable devotion to his family drives much of his behavior. S.H.I.E.L.D. and the Inhumans attempt to forge an alliance against Hydra, but deep-rooted distrust and hidden agendas quickly fracture this fragile unity. Jiaying’s manipulation culminates in her assassinating Gonzales and framing S.H.I.E.L.D. as aggressors, sparking an open war between humans and Inhumans.

As these tensions escalate, Jiaying launches a plan to spread Terrigen—which bestows Inhuman powers but is lethal to regular humans—across the globe, using the Iliad aircraft carrier as her staging ground. Skye, torn between her birth family and S.H.I.E.L.D., ultimately resists Jiaying’s extremism. Key losses and betrayals mount: Raina is killed for attempting to warn of Jiaying’s true intentions, Bobbi Morse is gravely wounded by Ward and Agent 33’s vendetta, and trust within S.H.I.E.L.D. is tested as allies question each other’s loyalties. Cal, initially an antagonist, comes to regret Jiaying’s descent into fanaticism and helps Coulson’s team intervene.

The final confrontation sees Cal kill Jiaying to spare Skye the burden, ending the immediate threat of the Terrigen genocide. Cal is given a new, peaceful life via the T.A.H.I.T.I. program, losing memories of his past. S.H.I.E.L.D. survives but is deeply changed: its leadership wounded, its agents traumatized, and a new initiative—led by Skye and focusing on secret Inhuman operatives—takes shape. Meanwhile, Grant Ward, mourning the death of Agent 33, seizes control of the fractured remnants of Hydra, promising ongoing danger. Coulson, maimed but resolute, sets S.H.I.E.L.D. on a cautious path forward, aware that Inhumans and other threats must now be handled with unprecedented secrecy and care.
