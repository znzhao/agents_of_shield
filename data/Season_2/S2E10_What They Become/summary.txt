After the fall of S.H.I.E.L.D. and the rise of Hydra, Phil Coulson’s team has been hunted, infiltrated, and forced to confront fractures within their own ranks. The mysterious alien Obelisk—known as the Diviner—emerges as a deadly artifact, selecting certain individuals as 'worthy' while killing others. Coulson is haunted by compulsive alien carvings revealed to be a map to a hidden Kree city tied to both Skye’s mysterious origins and Hydra’s current ambitions. Hydra, led by the immortal Dr. Whitehall (formerly Reinhardt), partners with Skye’s unstable father Cal, both aiming to access the city for their own purposes. S.H.I.E.L.D. and Hydra race against each other to the ancient ruins beneath Puerto Rico, with Mack falling victim to possession after contact with the city's alien technology.

During a direct confrontation, Grant Ward betrays Hydra to reunite Skye with her father, only for Cal to reveal disturbing truths about Skye’s legacy and her mother’s murder at Whitehall’s hands. When Whitehall is killed by Coulson—robbing Cal of his vengeance—violent chaos erupts as Cal nearly kills Coulson, only stopped by Skye herself, revealed at last as his daughter 'Daisy.' In the tunnels, Raina and the possessed Mack reach the city’s hidden temple, trailed by Skye and Trip. When the Diviner is triggered, a wave of transformation petrifies Raina and Skye but kills Trip. Skye emerges unharmed but changed, with new seismic powers, while Raina’s transformation is visibly monstrous. The aftermath leaves the team shattered by loss and uncertainty, the city’s secret unleashed, and Skye’s true nature—and destiny—finally awakened.
