In the aftermath of S.H.I.E.L.D.'s fall, Coulson leads a diminished but determined team, relentlessly hunted by the world and the resurgent Hydra. The alien Obelisk has emerged at the center of a global power struggle, coveted by Hydra's Daniel Whitehall and linked to Skye's mysterious lineage and to the obsessive alien engravings infecting Coulson's mind. Skye grapples with revelations from Grant Ward—her Hydra-agent ex-ally—about her true origins and her father's violent past, deepening her uncertainty and mistrust as Coulson grows more secretive under the strain of the alien compulsions.

Bobbi Morse rescues Simmons from her dangerous undercover mission inside Hydra, arming S.H.I.E.L.D. with crucial intelligence but forcing Simmons into open conflict. Fitz, still recovering from his brain trauma, slowly adapts and finds support from new ally Mack. The internal fractures within S.H.I.E.L.D. run deep: Ward escapes custody after handing over his manipulative brother to S.H.I.E.L.D., sowing doubt and stirring unfinished trauma. Meanwhile, Hydra grows bolder, openly attacking former agents and allies, and using deception to undermine S.H.I.E.L.D.'s already tattered reputation.

After a series of ritualistic murders targeting those connected to the alien writing, Coulson, Skye, and the team discover the victims are former S.H.I.E.L.D. agents who, like Coulson, survived the T.A.H.I.T.I. project using the alien GH.325 serum. Coulson pushes himself to the brink to unlock blocked memories of the project, realizing that the mental breakdowns and compulsions stem from alien genetic memories forcing the survivors to seek meaning in the strange symbols. The investigation leads Coulson to confront fellow survivor Sebastian Derik, culminating in the breakthrough that the mysterious carvings are not a map but blueprints to a hidden city—deepening the mystery even as the race to discover its purpose accelerates. The team remains fractured by unresolved loyalties, Ward's shadowy vendetta, and the looming threat of Hydra's grand designs.
