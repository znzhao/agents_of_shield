S.H.I.E.L.D. remains fractured in the wake of its collapse and Hydra’s infiltration, with leadership tensions between Phil Coulson and Robert Gonzales. Skye, now transformed into Daisy Johnson with earthquake powers, discovers her true heritage as the daughter of Jiaying, the Inhuman leader of the isolated Afterlife sanctuary, and the emotionally unstable Cal. As Hydra intensifies experiments on Gifteds, S.H.I.E.L.D. and Inhumans become reluctant allies to rescue their kin. Lingering mistrust, particularly concerning Grant Ward’s betrayals and the shadowy Theta Protocol project, undermines unity among the agents, as does growing fear and suspicion around powered individuals.

After S.H.I.E.L.D. successfully rescues Deathlok and Lincoln from Hydra, the two S.H.I.E.L.D. factions unite. Coulson is reinstated as director under the watchful eye of Gonzales and his council. Meanwhile, Raina’s precognitive powers pinpoint S.H.I.E.L.D.'s possession of an ancient Kree weapon—a black, morphing stone once used against Inhumans. Jiaying grows increasingly paranoid about S.H.I.E.L.D.'s intentions, while Raina warns that Jiaying may endanger their people and tries, unsuccessfully, to broker direct communication with S.H.I.E.L.D. Tensions boil over as Gonzales attempts peaceful negotiations with Jiaying, only to be murdered by her using a Terrigen crystal. Jiaying frames S.H.I.E.L.D. for violence against the Inhumans, shooting herself to stage an attack, and declares war, leaving Skye caught between her two families as the threat of open conflict looms.

Amid these machinations, key mysteries persist: the full purpose and scale of the Theta Protocol; the true capabilities and intentions of Inhuman leadership; the fate of Ward, who has gone rogue; and the repercussions of Hydra’s research. Former loyalties are tested, with Mack leaving S.H.I.E.L.D. in protest of Coulson’s leadership, Simmons displaying new ruthlessness, and relationships within S.H.I.E.L.D.'s core group shaken. As S.H.I.E.L.D. braces for escalation with the Inhumans and as ancient secrets surface, both worlds stand on the precipice of war.
