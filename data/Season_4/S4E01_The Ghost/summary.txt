The threat of Hive forced S.H.I.E.L.D. into a desperate struggle for survival. Daisy, once their trusted agent, was manipulated into betraying her team, only to be freed through Lash’s intervention at great cost. Hive intended to remake humanity as Primitives through a global release of a pathogen, but the team’s unity and sacrifice — most notably Lincoln’s heroic demise as he destroyed the warhead and Hive in space — averted the catastrophe. Daisy, traumatized by her actions and Lincoln’s death, fled S.H.I.E.L.D., becoming a guilt-ridden vigilante pursued by her former allies. S.H.I.E.L.D. itself became government-run and more tightly regulated, with a new, unseen director instituting strict protocols and lie detection to sniff out threats from within. Fitz and Simmons, now further committed to one another, take on expanded roles, with Simmons gaining greater influence within the organization. Dr. Radcliffe, with Fitz’s wary assistance, began clandestinely developing Aida, an advanced android designed to serve as a decoy and protector for agents — a secret kept even from Simmons.

In Los Angeles, Daisy (now operating as "Quake") hunts weapon-trafficking gangs connected to an eerie string of deaths and becomes embroiled in encounters with the vengeful, supernatural Ghost Rider, who metes out brutal justice to those he deems deserving, asserting a punishment much deeper than death. Coulson, Mack, and May pursue Daisy covertly, fearful of what S.H.I.E.L.D.'s new leadership might do if she’s found. Their investigation uncovers bizarre supernatural phenomena: gang members dying in apparent terror, a mysterious box, and spectral forces driving people to madness and violence. May herself is affected by a ghostly apparition. Daisy, wracked by guilt and physically deteriorating from overusing her powers, seeks out Ghost Rider and, after a brutal confrontation, begs him to end her suffering; he declines, leaving her fate unresolved. With Daisy operating alone, new threats looming, and old wounds unhealed, the team faces a shifting supernatural landscape and deep uncertainty about their roles and future.
