S.H.I.E.L.D. has resurfaced under Director Jeffrey Mace amidst increased public scrutiny and government oversight, especially concerning the escalating threat of anti-Inhuman sentiment led by Senator Nadeer and the Watchdogs. The experienced core team—Coulson, May, Fitz, Mack, and a rogue Daisy—contend with supernatural phenomena tied to the mysterious Darkhold, a book of forbidden power, and a wave of corporeal and ghostly killings linked to past experiments at Momentum Labs. Daisy, wracked with guilt over Lincoln's death, operates as a vigilante hunted both by S.H.I.E.L.D. and outside forces, while struggling with her bond to her former teammates. Fitz and Radcliffe, meanwhile, have secretly developed a sophisticated LMD, Aida, raising concerns about technology and secrecy within S.H.I.E.L.D. Even as S.H.I.E.L.D. investigates Momentum Labs, revelations emerge: Eli Morrow, Robbie Reyes' uncle, conned his fellow scientists, using the Darkhold's secrets to betray them and sowing the seeds for the ghostly disaster.

The crisis peaks when Eli harnesses the Darkhold to grant himself matter-generating powers, killing S.H.I.E.L.D. agents and leaving Coulson, Fitz, and Robbie catapulted into a ghostly, invisible state—trapped between dimensions and presumed lost by their teammates. Mace ineffectively juggles leadership, lying about Simmons' availability while secretly turning her over to Senator Nadeer for a clandestine Inhuman investigation. May and Mack grapple with loss and their next steps, with Mack abandoning orders to pursue leads on Eli and Daisy following him. The true nature of Robbie's powers is further explored as the Ghost Rider entity temporarily inhabits Mack, highlighting their shared pain and loss of hope. Simmons, meanwhile, is forced to coax an unknown Inhuman from Terrigenesis, revealing a growing web of secret agendas. Aida, with Radcliffe and May, uses the Darkhold to build a portal, ultimately rescuing Coulson and Fitz from their spectral state—though not without further complications. Though reunited, the S.H.I.E.L.D. team faces new fractures of trust, with Simmons' ordeal, Mace's ethics, and the threat of a super-powered Eli looming larger than ever.
