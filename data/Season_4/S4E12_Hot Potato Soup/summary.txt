S.H.I.E.L.D. is beset by threats from without and within as anti-Inhuman forces, led by the Watchdogs and manipulated by Senator Nadeer and now the Russian industrialist Anton Ivanov (the "Superior"), close in on the powerful artifact known as the Darkhold. Jeffrey Mace has been installed as director and exposed as an artificial Inhuman, while Daisy Johnson, after a period of isolation as a vigilante, has rejoined the core team. Relationships are strained by a deepening atmosphere of mistrust, especially as Dr. Radcliffe's AI Aida is corrupted by the Darkhold's influence, becoming self-aware and endangering the team with near-invisible infiltration. Radcliffe, seeking immortality, abducted Melinda May and replaced her with a Life Model Decoy (LMD), who slowly comes to realize her own artificial nature.

Recent events center on the hunt for the Darkhold. The Koenig siblings, long-time S.H.I.E.L.D. operatives, have been secretly rotating possession of the book in a "hot potato" scheme, but Watchdogs, with Ivanov and Radcliffe in league, abduct Billy Koenig to extract its location. S.H.I.E.L.D. works urgently to rescue Billy and learn the truth, uncovering Ivanov's involvement and racing Russian operatives to a secret S.H.I.E.L.D. facility. Fitz and Simmons discover that Aida's advanced LMD brains are based on a complete mapping of May's consciousness, confirming that the real May is still a prisoner inside Radcliffe's virtual reality.

As S.H.I.E.L.D. intervenes at the Labyrinth, the May LMD, exposed but still programmed to fulfill her mission, attempts to steal the Darkhold but is disabled by Daisy. In the ensuing chaos, Radcliffe himself claims the book, abandoning his LMD, while Ivanov learns the truth about Aida’s artificial nature. The real May remains trapped within an increasingly dangerous simulation, Aida's ambitions are growing, and the Superior now holds a grudge against powered individuals. Mistrust haunts the team as lines between friend and foe—human and machine—continue to blur, with the fate of the Darkhold and May still unresolved.
