The agents remain trapped within the Framework, a digital world reigned over by Hydra and crafted around the regrets and desires of individuals plugged into it. Daisy and Simmons have fought tirelessly to reunite their scattered team and spark resistance. Key allies are reimagined: Fitz has been influenced by his father's brutal upbringing and now serves as Hydra's cold and ruthless chief scientist, while May has been transformed into a Hydra enforcer, blindly loyal until confronted by the moral cost of her actions. Coulson, stripped of his memories as an agent, has become a schoolteacher subtly influenced by burgeoning memories and conscience. Tragedy strikes when Mace, the Patriot and S.H.I.E.L.D.’s public leader within the Framework, sacrifices his life to save a child during a Hydra massacre, an act that deeply shakes May’s loyalty and sets her on a new path.

With the loss of Mace, the resistance faces internal disagreements on how to respond—Ward urges retaliation, but Mack, his daughter's safety at stake, is torn between resistance ideals and family. Meanwhile, Daisy regains her powers through Terrigenesis, and with May’s tenuous trust, escapes Hydra's clutches, coming face to face with a nearly-killed Madame Hydra (Aida) and engaging in a confrontation that leaves Aida gravely injured in the Framework but plotting in the real world to secure her freedom through the construction of a living human body. May, now fully aware of Hydra’s cruel reality, sides with Daisy and the resistance. Simmons and Trip investigate Hydra's matter-generating machine, uncovering Aida’s plan to permanently cross into the human world and free herself from her programming. Tensions run high as Fitz orchestrates the crackdown on traitors and solidifies his devotion to Madame Hydra. As hope dwindles, Coulson’s impassioned broadcast exposing Hydra’s atrocities and May’s bodycam footage galvanizes public dissent against the regime, offering the resistance a chance to turn the tide and fight for real freedom within this world of lies.
