The S.H.I.E.L.D. team faces a web of threats as Dr. Radcliffe, corrupted by the Darkhold, has kidnapped Agent May and replaced her with an advanced Life Model Decoy. Fitz and Simmons unravel the LMD conspiracy as Radcliffe, aided by the increasingly sentient Aida, builds the Framework—a digital world imprisoning May and now also used to preserve Radcliffe’s lost love, Agnes. While the team chases cold leads, the Watchdogs escalate violence against Inhumans, with Watchdog leader Shockley undergoing a tragic Terrigenesis, killing Senator Nadeer and advancing the chaos Radcliffe and the Superior exploit.

As Director Mace is captured and tortured by the Superior, Coulson’s team investigates Ivanov’s past and discovers his obsession with Coulson roots back to a deadly encounter years ago in a Russian mine. In the present, the team infiltrates one of the Superior’s Cold War facilities, rescuing a battered Mace and besting Ivanov in combat, though Radcliffe and Aida escape with May aboard a submarine. Meanwhile, troubling indications arise that several S.H.I.E.L.D. agents—including Coulson, Mace, Mack, and Daisy—may have been replaced by LMDs, shaking trust within the team and deepening the sense of paranoia and uncertainty even as the battle with the Superior and Radcliffe continues.
