S.H.I.E.L.D. has reemerged under the Inhuman Director Jeffrey Mace, facing new oversight while dealing with both human and supernatural threats. Phil Coulson has returned to field work, joined by Mack and Melinda May, as they investigate mysterious ghostly entities linked to Momentum Labs and the ancient, forbidden book called the Darkhold. Daisy Johnson has gone rogue as Quake, guilt-ridden and self-destructive after the loss of Lincoln, and is hunted both by S.H.I.E.L.D. and the anti-Inhuman Watchdogs. Meanwhile, Fitz and Dr. Radcliffe have secretly created Aida, an advanced Life Model Decoy, whose existence is hidden from the new director. Senator Nadeer continues to rally public sentiment against Inhumans, posing a political threat.

The supernatural crisis intensifies as Lucy Bauer—now a ghostly specter—and the other science "ghosts" seek to regain corporeal form using the power of the Darkhold, killing along the way. The connection between Momentum Labs’ failed experiment, the Darkhold, and Eli Morrow (Robbie Reyes' uncle) is revealed: Eli was involved in the original experiments and harbors his own ambitions regarding the book's power. Ghost Rider, Robbie Reyes, joins uneasy forces with S.H.I.E.L.D. after his vengeance-driven path intersects with the agency's mission, but his true nature and origins remain disturbing to his loved ones. Daisy and S.H.I.E.L.D. rescue Robbie’s paraplegic brother, Gabe, keeping him in the dark about Robbie’s demonic alter ego.

As the true past of Momentum Labs unravels, it's revealed that Eli Morrow betrayed his colleagues, using the Darkhold’s secrets for his own gain and transforming them into vengeful ghosts. Amidst a race to stop Lucy from harnessing the Darkhold during a dangerous experiment, S.H.I.E.L.D. and the Ghost Rider confront her at an abandoned Roxxon facility. Daisy and Mack attempt to foil the experiment while Coulson, Fitz, and Robbie face the final showdown inside. Ultimately, Eli uses the Darkhold’s power to complete the experiment himself, gaining new, mysterious power, and in the process causes Coulson, Fitz, and Robbie to vanish in a burst of white light—leaving their fates and the threat of the Darkhold unresolved, while relationships among the team grow more strained and the dangers they face become ever more complex.
