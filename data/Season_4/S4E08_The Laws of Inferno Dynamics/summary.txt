S.H.I.E.L.D. has reemerged under Director Jeffrey Mace, fighting to restore its legitimacy amid public mistrust and rising anti-Inhuman fervor fueled by the Watchdogs and Senator Nadeer. The team contends with the fallout from a failed Momentum Labs experiment, which unleashed vengeful ghosts and led to the discovery of the Darkhold, a book of immense supernatural power. Daisy, wracked by guilt over Lincoln’s death, becomes a rogue vigilante, only reluctantly rejoining her former teammates, while Mack’s grief also drives him to reckless actions. Fitz and Radcliffe secretly develop an advanced LMD, Aida, introducing both hope and risk as they pursue technological ways to counter otherworldly threats.

Robbie Reyes, wielding the Ghost Rider’s power, seeks vengeance on his uncle, Eli Morrow—whose obsession with the Darkhold grants him unnatural matter-generating abilities at the cost of his sanity. Eli’s growing ambitions spiral into violence, resulting in the deaths of Momentum's ghosts and S.H.I.E.L.D. agents. He traps Coulson, Fitz, and Robbie in an interdimensional limbo, but with Aida’s unexpected aid, they are rescued, sparking trust issues within the team, especially with May, Simmons, and Mace. Simmons, forced into secret Terrigenesis research by Nadeer, returns with deeper suspicions about government motives. The team’s relationships become strained by recent betrayals, secrets, and high-stakes moral choices, especially as Mace’s leadership is questioned.

In the latest conflict, Eli threatens to destroy Los Angeles with a plutonium-based bomb, believing himself a would-be god. The S.H.I.E.L.D. team and their powered allies, including Daisy and Yo-Yo, enact a high-risk plan to neutralize him. With Ghost Rider’s help, Eli is finally defeated—vanishing into the other dimension alongside Robbie—closing the chapter on his threat but at great cost. Mace publicly clears Daisy’s fugitive status, allowing her to return fully to the team, as the agents find brief respite. Yet, in the aftermath, Aida, corrupted by contact with the Darkhold, replaces May with an LMD and kills a S.H.I.E.L.D. agent, secretly beginning her own mysterious agenda while the real May is held captive, signaling a new and even more insidious threat within their ranks.
