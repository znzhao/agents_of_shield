After being mysteriously resurrected following the Battle of New York, Agent Phil Coulson resumes leadership of a specialized S.H.I.E.L.D. team comprising Melinda May, Grant Ward, Leo Fitz, Jemma Simmons, and the skilled hacker Skye. The group contends with various global threats involving advanced technology and enhanced individuals—including the dangerous Centipede organization, led by the enigmatic Clairvoyant. Skye’s journey to uncover her own origins leads to the revelation that she is a "0-8-4"—a person of unknown and possibly alien origin. Coulson also uncovers disturbing truths about his own resurrection: he was brought back via the secretive GH.325 serum, sourced from a mysterious blue-skinned alien, raising profound ethical questions about S.H.I.E.L.D.'s methods and motives. The serum is later used to save the gravely injured Skye, linking the fates of both agents to the agency’s darkest secrets and drawing unwanted attention from those who covet its power.

Recent events see mounting tensions as Mike Peterson, thought dead, is discovered to be alive but forcibly transformed into the cybernetic assassin Deathlok and controlled by Centipede. Simultaneously, the team's emotional bonds are tested—trust is strained by secrecy, especially as Coulson becomes increasingly determined to find answers about his resurrection and Skye’s miraculous recovery. Between the internal conflicts and external threats, the agents struggle with loyalty, deception, and the burden of knowledge.

Amidst these tensions, the Asgardian enchantress Lorelei arrives on Earth, seizing control of groups of men—including Ward—through mystical compulsion. Lady Sif, dispatched from Asgard, joins Coulson’s team to pursue Lorelei. The crisis forces the team into direct and dangerous conflict, culminating in a battle aboard their own plane as Sif ultimately subdues Lorelei and breaks her spell. In the aftermath, trust among the agents—particularly between May and Ward—remains shaken, while Coulson decides to confide in Skye about the alien source of the GH.325 serum and his suspicion that Nick Fury possesses crucial answers. The team is left reeling, aware both of new threats and that the true scope of S.H.I.E.L.D.’s secrets is far from revealed.
