Following the Battle of New York, Agent Phil Coulson is resurrected under mysterious circumstances and assembles a specialized S.H.I.E.L.D. team to investigate emerging superhuman threats and anomalies tied to the fallout of alien invasions and secret experiments. The roster includes the capable but emotionally scarred Melinda May, the secretive and skilled Grant Ward, brilliant science partners Fitz and Simmons, and the resourceful hacker Skye, whose search for her true origins and family remains a point of personal motivation and unresolved mystery. Their missions bring them into direct conflict with dangerous groups like Centipede, whose experiments with Extremis and other alien technologies create unstable super-soldiers, highlighting both S.H.I.E.L.D.'s ethical struggles and the escalating threat landscape.

Over time, the team’s bond grows through close calls, including survival against enhanced adversaries, May facing her haunted past as "The Cavalry," Simmons’ brush with death from an alien virus, and Skye slowly proving herself as a valued agent. Ward and May form a secretive but tense romantic relationship, while Coulson and Skye's personal quests—to uncover the truth about his resurrection and her past—intertwine with their professional duties. Coulson becomes increasingly disturbed by hints that his own revival involved more than he’s been told.

Centipede's threat escalates as their super-soldiers break Edison Po, a key Centipede mastermind, out of prison. With regular agents outmatched, the team recruits Mike Peterson—one of their earliest enhanced contacts—now stabilized by S.H.I.E.L.D. and eager to prove himself. Investigations reveal the mysterious figure called "The Clairvoyant" steering Centipede’s operations from the shadows. When the team confronts Centipede’s soldiers, a deadly trap leaves one soldier dead to protect Centipede’s secrets and hints that Mike himself is key to their next phase. Amid mounting external attacks and internal strains—May pushing Skye away, Skye’s heartbreaking setbacks in her parent search, and the ever-present shadow of "The Clairvoyant"—the team stands united but vulnerable, with personal and organizational stakes mounting as these mysteries deepen.
