After Agent Phil Coulson is mysteriously resurrected, he is put in charge of a new S.H.I.E.L.D. team, including agents May, Ward, Fitz, Simmons, and hacker Skye. Their early missions uncover a clandestine program called Centipede, involving the use of alien technology and super-soldier serums, and entangle them with the enigmatic Clairvoyant. Skye’s mysterious past is revealed to be connected to an alien-derived serum, GH-325, the same substance used to bring Coulson back to life. As secrets and surveillance within the team breed mistrust—particularly surrounding May's loyalty and Skye's hacking—the threat of Hydra grows.

The collapse of S.H.I.E.L.D. is triggered by the revelation that John Garrett, a high-level S.H.I.E.L.D. agent, is the Clairvoyant and leader of Hydra within the organization. The team's trust is irreparably damaged when Ward, previously a trusted team member, is unmasked as Garrett's double agent after assassinating colleagues and abducting Skye. With the agency in ruins and Hydra unleashing chaos—including mass breakouts at the Fridge—the surviving team members become fugitives, seeking refuge at Providence base. May leaves after feeling her loyalty questioned, while Skye is taken by Ward, who is hunting for encrypted S.H.I.E.L.D. secrets. Meanwhile, the fate and motives of Fury, and the truth behind Project T.A.H.I.T.I., remain shadowy.

In the wake of Koenig's murder and Skye's abduction, the team’s desperation peaks. Skye cleverly signals for help and confronts Ward about his Hydra allegiance, refusing to be manipulated. With the intervention of Maria Hill and renewed focus from Coulson and his fractured team, Skye is rescued—thanks in part to her quick thinking and sabotage of the hard drive Hydra covets. May, independently seeking the truth, discovers Fury left a final report about T.A.H.I.T.I. for her to find. As the team narrowly escapes military capture and regroups, their unity is fragile but their resolve to confront Hydra and save S.H.I.E.L.D.'s legacy is stronger than ever. The true mastermind and lingering secrets, especially regarding Fury's fate and Coulson's resurrection, remain unresolved, propelling them toward an uncertain confrontation with Garrett and Hydra.
