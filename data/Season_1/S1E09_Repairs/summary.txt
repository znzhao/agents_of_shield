Following the Battle of New York, Agent Phil Coulson is miraculously revived and tasked with leading a new S.H.I.E.L.D. team on a mobile command jet. His team—a mix of skilled agents and young specialists—includes combat ace Melinda May, stoic but capable field agent Grant Ward, engineering savants Fitz and Simmons, and the gifted hacker Skye, whose search for her birth parents remains unresolved. The group faces threats from the shadowy Centipede project, which uses risky alien technology to create super-powered soldiers, reflecting the ethical complexities of S.H.I.E.L.D.'s global operations and the toll this work takes on its agents. Early missions forge bonds as the team overcomes deadly traps, Simmons' near-fatal exposure to an alien virus, and attacks by enhanced individuals, which highlight S.H.I.E.L.D.'s secrecy and willingness to make personal sacrifices for the greater good.

Personal journeys deepen as the team learns to trust one another. Ward and May, each haunted by past trauma, grow closer, even becoming romantically involved following their confrontation with an Asgardian artifact that forces Ward to relive childhood abuse. Fitz and Simmons strengthen their partnership and resilience through shared danger. Coulson grows increasingly suspicious about the true nature of his "Tahiti" resurrection, while Skye slowly earns her place as a valued team member, though she remains determined to learn the truth about her origins.

Recently, the team investigated a supposed telekinetic, Hannah Hutchins, whose perceived powers manifest after a traumatic explosion. Through investigation, they uncover Tobias Ford—an accident victim caught between dimensions, primarily attempting to protect rather than harm Hannah. May, drawing on her own experience dealing with "gifted" threats, ultimately resolves the situation, helping both Ford and Hannah find peace. The incident prompts reflection on how S.H.I.E.L.D. supports superhumans and deepens understanding of May's haunted past as "The Cavalry," with Skye gaining empathy for her. The team's unity and trust continue to grow, but mysteries about Centipede, Coulson's true resurrection story, and Skye's origins remain unresolved, keeping tensions simmering beneath their strengthening camaraderie.
