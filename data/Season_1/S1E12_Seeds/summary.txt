After the Battle of New York, Agent Phil Coulson is mysteriously resurrected and tasked by S.H.I.E.L.D. to lead an elite team—Melinda May, Grant Ward, Fitz, Simmons, and hacker Skye. Their mission is to confront threats involving superhuman abilities, advanced tech, and organizations like Centipede, whose schemes tie into illegal experimentation and Coulson's own revival. Tensions frequently run high, as Skye probes her unknown heritage and the secret behind Coulson's resurrection, while the team navigates internal mistrust, hidden agendas, and S.H.I.E.L.D.'s shadowy bureaucracy. Major confrontations with Centipede, led by the ambiguous Clairvoyant, place them in mortal danger, especially after Coulson is kidnapped. He endures psychological torture to force a recollection of his resurrection, ultimately learning of S.H.I.E.L.D.'s excruciating medical intervention to bring him back, acting under direct orders from Nick Fury.

Recently, as the team regroups from Centipede's attack, the emotional cost of S.H.I.E.L.D.'s secrets leaves deep fissures among them. The fate of S.H.I.E.L.D. experiment Mike Peterson is uncertain. Coulson investigates Skye's origins, discovering from former agent Richard Lumley that Skye was classified as an "0-8-4"—an object of unknown origin—for whom an entire Chinese village was wiped out. Skye was protected via a series of false orphanage placements, and violence haunts anyone investigating her past. Coulson chooses to reveal these painful truths to Skye, deepening their bond but burdening her with her dark legacy. Meanwhile, at the S.H.I.E.L.D. academy, Fitz and Simmons uncover a plot by prodigy Donnie Gill and peer Seth, who, with outside encouragement from industrialist Ian Quinn, build a freezing device that spirals out of control and leads to disaster. While the immediate threat is neutralized—the loss of Seth and Donnie's escape—Quinn's manipulations and the lure of S.H.I.E.L.D. technology in the wrong hands remain, and the full extent of Centipede and the Clairvoyant's plans is unresolved.
