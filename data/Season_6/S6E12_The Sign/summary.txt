After the death of Phil Coulson, S.H.I.E.L.D. faces a bizarre and existential threat with the arrival of Sarge, a man with Coulson’s face but an unknown origin. Sarge leads a team in pursuit of Izel, an ancient non-corporeal being. Izel’s agenda becomes clear: harnessing the energy of the Monoliths to open a gateway and bring her kind into this world, using possessed hosts (including agency personnel) and the deadly, hive-like Shrike creatures to prepare the way. Sarge’s true nature is ultimately revealed — he is Pachakutiq, a being created from the Monoliths' energy, sharing Coulson’s memories but not his soul, making his identity battle central to the escalating conflict.

As Izel seizes control of the portal temple and forcibly possesses key figures, her threat becomes global. Mack, Yo-Yo, and Flint are taken captive, with Flint’s geokinesis used to rebuild the Monoliths even as he tries to resist. The S.H.I.E.L.D. team rapidly innovates: Deke creates anti-possession wristbands and attempts a bold rescue with FitzSimmons’ help. May, Daisy, and Sarge lead the charge against Izel, with Daisy betting on Sarge’s fragmented humanity to tip the scales. May confesses her hope that Sarge can be separated from Pachakutiq’s evil, longing to regain something of Coulson. Inside the temple, as Izel opens the gateway, Sarge’s internal struggle climaxes. Manipulated by Izel, he impales May and casts her into the portal, choosing to purge the vestiges of his humanity—leaving May’s fate and the fate of the world on the brink of catastrophe.
