After the death of Phil Coulson, S.H.I.E.L.D. confronted a mysterious threat in the form of Sarge—who bears Coulson’s likeness but has an alien origin tied to the Monoliths. Sarge leads a ruthless team to pursue Izel, a non-corporeal being intent on breaching dimensions and allowing her kind to invade Earth. Izel manipulates her way into the ancient temple and forces Flint to reconstruct the Monoliths, using the portal's power to attempt her plan. S.H.I.E.L.D. grapples with internal threats, possession by Izel and her Shrike creatures, and the catastrophic potential of the Monolith gateway. Deke, Fitz, Simmons, and others develop countermeasures and launch perilous rescue operations, but the struggle leaves the team fractured and the fate of the world uncertain as Izel's ritual nears completion.

Meanwhile, the Chronicom Hunters, led by Malachi, initiate their own assault on S.H.I.E.L.D., exploiting knowledge extracted from FitzSimmons’ minds. Their goal is to eliminate S.H.I.E.L.D. and transform Earth into their new world, Chronyca-3. On all fronts the team is beleaguered, with beloved agents killed, overtaken, or forced into impossible choices. May is left mortally wounded by Sarge, who ultimately rejects his remaining humanity and reveals himself as Pachakutiq—a being created from Monolith energy. Despite May’s apparent death, she awakens in an alien realm, resists Izel, and intervenes in the final confrontation at the temple. With Daisy and her team’s aid, May helps defeat both Izel and Pachakutiq at great personal cost, ending the existential threat.

The battle leaves S.H.I.E.L.D. deeply wounded: May is gravely injured, Yo-Yo narrowly survives a Shrike infection, and the Chronicoms remain a looming threat, armed with S.H.I.E.L.D.’s secrets and technology. FitzSimmons, aided by the disguised Enoch, are given a mysterious chance to change their fates in the face of mounting dangers. With Izel and Pachakutiq vanquished but the Chronicoms advancing, the future of S.H.I.E.L.D.—and the world—remains precarious, defined by sacrifice, resilience, and looming uncertainty.
