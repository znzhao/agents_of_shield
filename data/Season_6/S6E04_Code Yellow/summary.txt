After the catastrophic fallout from the battle with Graviton, S.H.I.E.L.D. is scattered across the stars and Earth. The loss of Fitz, presumed dead, devastates Simmons, who becomes obsessed with finding him. Daisy, Simmons, Piper, and Davis embark on a rogue space mission to search for Fitz, leading them to the lawless planet Kitson. Here, they experience a chaotic, alien-induced ordeal before Fitz is captured by the mysterious Malachi, separating him once again from his loved ones. Meanwhile, Fitz and Chronicom Enoch struggle to evade intergalactic pursuers, raising the stakes for everyone.

On Earth, Mack leads a fractured S.H.I.E.L.D., supported by May, Yo-Yo, and Agent Keller, while facing strange dimensional phenomena and the appearance of Sarge—a figure identical to Phil Coulson but coldly ruthless and hostile. Sarge commands a team executing violent attacks for enigmatic purposes, including stabbing victims with alien knives that cause deadly rock-like growths. As tensions rise over secret relationships and shifting power dynamics within S.H.I.E.L.D., Deke resurfaces in Silicon Valley, using his knowledge of alien tech to become a tech entrepreneur. Sarge and his crew target Deke, leading to a fierce confrontation at Deke's company: S.H.I.E.L.D. intervenes, but Sarge captures May as a hostage, deepening the mystery of his identity and motives. The team is left divided: Daisy and Simmons are still lost in space, Fitz's fate is uncertain, and those on Earth are now caught in a deadly game with a threat bearing Coulson's face.
